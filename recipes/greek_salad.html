Greek Salad

Description

With Greeks’ heavy use of vegetables on their dishes, this roundup wouldn’t be complete without a proper veggie salad.

You’ll only need seven ingredients to make this refreshing meal, and all of them are super easy to find at your local grocery store.

For a flavorful salad, you’ll need to make sure that the chopped ingredients are similar in size. This way, you’ll get a mix of veggies, cheese, and olives with each bite.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Red Onion
  • Oives
  • Feta Cheese
